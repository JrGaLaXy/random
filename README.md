# Museji

**Museji** is a piano and rhythm app that lets users play, compete, and connect with other pianists in real time. Inspired by popular piano apps and designed with feedback from real users, Museji focuses on skill-based gameplay, social interaction, and fun rhythm mechanics.

## About

Museji was born from the desire to make piano practice more engaging and social. It combines interactive rhythm gameplay with real-time multiplayer, allowing players to play solo, in duets, or in fully customizable rooms with friends.

## Features

- **Free Play / Jam Rooms**: Play along with friends, create your own mini-orchestra with melody, harmony, bass, and drums.  
- **Versus Mode**: Challenge friends or players worldwide in real-time piano battles. Track accuracy, timing, and leaderboard rankings.  
- **Real-Time Feedback**: Immediate recognition of notes and rhythm for accurate practice.  
- **Multiplayer**: Sync with friends or global players in real time.  
- **Vast Song Library**: Learn thousands of songs across multiple genres.  
- **MIDI Keyboard Support**: Connect your own digital piano for authentic play.
